      Logo Usage Rules

      • Use the main logo whenever possible.
      • Use the secondary logo in reduced spaces.
      • Use black-and-white logo versions on busy backgrounds.
      • Use the tower icon as a special brand identifier.
      • Do not rotate, stretch, distort, outline, or modify the logo.

      Layout & Image Rules

      • Use transparency and thin lines to separate text from image backgrounds.
      • Maintain legibility and never lose image focus.
      • Do not mix heavy and light icon groups in the same composition.
      • Use restrained radii and premium editorial spacing.
